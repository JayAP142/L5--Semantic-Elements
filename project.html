Tourism in Ghana

Hundreds of tourist sites are scattered across Ghana. They attract tourists from around the globe to marvel at their majesty. From the Elmina Castle in the south to the Paga Crocodile Pond way up north, Ghana's tourist sites are a sight to behold.
